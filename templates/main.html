Frequently Asked Questions



How do I schedule a pickup?

To schedule a pickup, simply visit our website and click on the "Book a Pickup" button. Follow the instructions to provide your details and select a convenient time for pickup.

What items do you accept for scrap?

We accept a wide range of scrap items including metal, paper, plastic, and electronic waste. However, there may be restrictions on certain hazardous materials.

How soon can I expect payment after pickup?

Payment is typically processed within 1-2 business days after the pickup is completed. You will receive payment through your preferred payment method.

Is there a minimum quantity required for pickup?

While there is no strict minimum quantity requirement for pickup, we recommend scheduling a pickup for a reasonable amount of scrap to make the process efficient.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule my pickup?

If you need to cancel or reschedule your pickup, please contact our customer support team as soon as possible. We will do our best to accommodate your request.
